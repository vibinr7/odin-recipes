Meat Loaf

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's quite good!

Hands down, this was the best meatloaf that I've ever made. I used about 2.3 lbs. of ground beef. Following some of the advice of others as well as adding a few of my own tweaks

I made this meatloaf with my boyfriend. Not only was it delicious but we had a lot of fun making it. At first i was worried that it would be too onion-y, but using the whole onion was well worth it.

Ingredients

  1. 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  2. 1 egg
  3. 2 cups water
  4. 1 onion, chopped
  5. 1 cup milk
  6. 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  7. salt and pepper to taste
  8. 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  9. 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  10. ⅓ cup ketchup

steps

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  • In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  • Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
